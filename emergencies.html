FEATURES

If you have any Emergency

  • Request help through Voice Call, SOS, SMS, Email, Web Request and Panic buttons.
  • Automatic location identification of the caller/victim
  • Dynamic emergency response service from the nearest emergency response vehicle.
  • Live tracking of emergency response vehicles.
  • Emergency service coordination from a centralized control center.
  • Improves decision making in emergency situation which minimizes response time
    • In case of emergency call 112
    This Toll free number provides pre-hospital health services to patients through life support ambulances to nearby hospitals for further treatment. This includes patients suffering from road accidents, all critical diseases, critical pregnant women, neonates related diseases, patients suffering from an epidemic, natural calamities and manmade hazards, critical heart patients, snake bite, all accidents, food poisoning, respiratory diseases, brain diseases etc

FEATURES

Ambulance

  • “Golden Hour Theory” – Patient to be shifted within first hour to nearest hospital.
  • Available 24 X 7 and offer ‘free of cost’ service to the patient
  • Ambulances are well equipped 'Advance Life Support Ambulances' (ALS) and 'Basic Life Support Ambulances' (BLS).
  • Operated through trained drivers and trained EMSO’s (Emergency Medical Service Officers) who are Doctors.
    • Dial 108 for Ambulance
  • Includes Computer technology integration, voice logger system, GIS - Geographic Information System, GPS - Geographic Position System, AVLT - Automatic Vehicle Location System and MCS - Mobile Communication System
